Welcome.

Dear viewers,

A hearty welcome to the Sisters of St. Joseph of Saint-Marc! You will discover how unique we are, living our Charism and Spirituality in 7 different countries: France, Germany, India, Italy, USA, Philippines, and Ukraine. We hail from 9 different nationalities: French, German, Indian, Philippine, Ukrainian, Congolese, Madagascan, Slovakian, and American. Yes, we live in unity in diversity.

Our community thrives on shared values and mutual respect, fostering a deep sense of global sisterhood. Each sister brings her cultural richness, enhancing our collective mission to serve with love and compassion. Together, we embody a harmonious blend of traditions and modernity, dedicated to making a positive impact worldwide.


Fr. Pierre envisaged this community as a group of women living together in the spirit of Christ and the Holy Family in the service of the Church. We are devoted to promoting human dignity, fostering education, providing healthcare, and engaging in various social and pastoral activities.

Our charism centers on living in unity and love, inspired by the example of the Holy Family. We strive to be witnesses of Christ's love through our actions and presence in the world. Our commitment to prayer, community life, and service guides us in our mission to be instruments of peace and reconciliation in society.


Thank you for visiting our page, and we hope you feel inspired by our mission and work. May God bless you!

Our Mission.

Our mission is to embody and express the spirit of the Sisters of St. Joseph of Saint-Marc through dedicated service, fostering unity and reconciliation. We strive to uphold human dignity, provide compassionate care, and promote holistic development across diverse communities. Guided by the teachings of Christ and the Holy Family, we commit ourselves to building a world of love, peace, and justice.
